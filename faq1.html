How do I choose the right refrigerator for my home?

Choosing the right refrigerator depends on your needs and available space. Consider factors such as size, capacity, energy efficiency, and features like ice makers or water dispensers. It's important to measure the space where the refrigerator will be placed to ensure a proper fit.
